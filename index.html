About Me

A brief summary of my education background, work experience, life in general & what defines me.

Currently an Entrepreneur In Training(EIT) at MEST in Accra Ghana. A holder of BSc. Computer Science degree from JKUAT. Also a Certified Public Accountant(CPA)K graduate.

A co-founder of Chagua Charity, a social venture based in Kenya. Previously worked with Heinrich Boell Foundation & Lions of Science in Nairobi Kenya.

Integrity & Trust matters most to me. The ability to create value or solve a problem gets me motivated each day. Helping others is part of me.

My Interests

Over the weekends, I love spending time with friends, visiting new places, volunteering and Community Outreach activities. I love to cook while listening to house music.
